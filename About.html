About Mishkat

Learn more about our company, mission, and the passionate team behind our success

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Mishkat has been at the forefront of digital transformation, helping businesses across various industries streamline their operations and achieve their goals through innovative software solutions.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between complex business requirements and user-friendly technology solutions. Today, we're proud to be an official Odoo Partner, serving clients worldwide with comprehensive ERP systems, custom web applications, and mobile solutions.

2015: Company founded
2018: Became Official Odoo Partner
2024: 500+ Projects Completed

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative software solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and digital transformation. We strive to deliver excellence through cutting-edge technology, personalized service, and continuous innovation.

Our Vision

To become the leading software solutions provider in the region, recognized for our expertise, reliability, and commitment to client success. We envision a future where every business can harness the power of technology to achieve their full potential.
